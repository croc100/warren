# Warren Business & Vision

Warren is decentralizing internet infrastructure through a **four-pillar strategy** targeting the individual vs. government network conflict.

## The 4-Pillar Platform

### 1. ISP Marketplace — $50B+ TAM
Trade bandwidth peer-to-peer. Eliminate ISP monopoly. Revenue: 2-3% transaction fees.

### 2. Privacy Gateway — $10B+ TAM
Policy-transparent, data-encrypted routing. Legally defensible (governments see intent, not behavior). Revenue: B2B licensing ($2-5/user/month), B2C subscriptions.

### 3. Independence Logger — $500M-1B TAM
Cryptographic proof of censorship evasion on-chain. Revenue: NGO/journalist subscriptions ($20K-100K/year).

### 4. Satellite Fallback — $20B+ TAM
Auto-failover to Starlink/Kuiper + LoRa mesh when ground internet fails. Revenue: Premium tier (+$5/month), enterprise ($500K+/year).

**Total TAM: $80B+ (2030 projection)**

---

## Why This Works

| Problem | Solution | Why It Matters |
|---------|----------|---|
| **ISP chokepoint** (90% flows through 3-5 ISPs/country) | Decentralized marketplace | Economic incentive: individuals profit |
| **VPN bans** (China, Russia, Iran) | Privacy-first protocol (transparent + encrypted) | Legally defensible: governments have less reason to ban |
| **Censorship tracking** (no audit trail) | Cryptographic proof on-chain | Independence: activists can prove evasion |
| **Infrastructure collapse** (network outages) | Satellite + mesh auto-failover | Resilience: no more single points of failure |

---

## Competitive Advantages

1. **Network effects** — Each user makes network stronger for all
2. **Regulatory defensibility** — Unlike VPNs, our model is harder to ban
3. **Ecosystem lock-in** — Once users configure policies + run nodes, switching cost is prohibitive
4. **Horizontal expansion** — Warren can expand to DNS, email, storage (network as backbone)
